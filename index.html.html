CONSTITUTION AND BY-LAWS

PREAMBLE

We,are the L.E.T.S Band students of ISU San Mateo Campus,concious of the vital role of the youth in nation building,until ourselves in order to establish an Organization that shall ensure solidarity of our common endeavor and support the University in the fulfillment of its goals for a better Philippines society,with the help of the Almighty God, to ordain and promulgate this constitution and By-Laws.

Article I

NAME,OFFICE AND NATURE

Section 1.

The Organization shall be called LETS Band.It shall be domicilled inside and outside the Isabela State University-San Mateo Campus.

Section 2.

The LETS Band shall be an academic,non-profit and non-secretarial organization

Section 3

The LETS Band shall be independent organization,under the Student Body Organization of the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Article II

OBJECTIVES

Section 1

To promote the spirit of a cooperation and fellowship among its members and with another student organization. Section 2.

To devote more time and effort to productive activities.

Section 3.

To provide activities to members concerning their academic problems.

Article III

MEMBERSHIP AND DUES

Section 1.

All members of the LETS Band are automatically member of the organization,provided they adhere to ideals of these Constitution and By-Laws

Section2.

LETS Band Organization shall need to collect money from each member as their membership fee.The amount of money to be collected shall be 50.00 pesos only every semester.

Article IV

RIGHTS AND PREVILLEGES OF MEMBERS

Section1

All members of LETS Band shall be considered as active and participating members such as,shall be entitled to the rights privilleges and benefits hereinafter provided.

Section 2.

All members shall be informed of all the affairs,policies,program,projects and actions adopted by the LETS Band Organization.

Article V

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

Section 1.

All members must observe respect and uphold the constitution and by-Laws of LETS Band Organization.

Section 2.

All members must abide by the policies,decisions and resolution adopted by the LETS Band Organization.

Section 3.

All members must attend and actively participate in all activitries of the LETS Band Organization.

Article VI

GENERAL ASSEMBLE

Section 1.

The general assembly shall be composed of all members of the LETS Band Organization and shall be the Highest policy-making the body of the LETS Band Organization.

Section 2.

The general assembly shall convene once a year to elect officers and to adopt policies,programsa and project that should be implemented and act on such other organization matter as may be forward.

Section 3.

The general assembly may convene in a special session upon the recommendation of the President with the concurrence of the Executive Committee.

Article VII

EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

Section 1

The executive committee shall be the second policy-making body and the supreme governing body of the LETS Band Organization.When the general assembly is not in session.

Section 2.

The executive committee shall be the implemnting body to executive programs,actions and policies adopted by the LETS Band Organization.

Section 3.

The executive committee shall be composed of the following offices.

PRESIDENT:NATHANIEL S.TAGUBA

VICE PRESIDENT:JERICO M.MENDOZA

SECRETARY:CHRISTINE ROSE A.FLORES

TREASURER:CHERRYGAY G.OLIGO

AUDITOR:MELODY JANE DOMINGO

PIO:SERGE DE GUZMAN

BUSINESS MANAGER:KEVIN CARLO D.MESANA and RED N.VILLANUEVA

Section 4

The Executive Committee shall have the following Duties and Functions.

A.To sponsors activities which will promote objectives of the LETS Band Oganization for the approval of the General Assembly.

B.To act,on behalf General Assembly,on matters that require immediate action.

C.To supervise and coordinate the project and activities of the LETS Band Organization.

D.To prepare the placce schedule of agenda for all regular and all special meetings of the LETS Band Organization.

Section 5.

The executive committee shall meet twice a month or as often as deemed as necessary.

Section 6.

Any offers who fails to attend any programs or regular meeting set by the President should have fines.

Section 7.

Any offers who fails to attend three consecutive sessions without the executive committee the reason for his absence,shall automatically be dropped from his position.

Article VIII

POWERS,DUTIES AND FUNCTIONS OFFICERS

Section 1.

The Presedent shall have the following powers,duties and functions:

a.Preside over the meeting of the Executive Committee and the session of general assembly

b.Appoint Representative with the consent of the executive committee upon the recommended of the students from their respective levels.

c.See that organization is being run conformity with the policies set forth in this constitution and by-laws.

d.See that the duties of officers as set forth in the constitution and by-laws,other rules and regulation which may use from time to time.

e.Perform such other duties as his office may require or as may delegated to hime the general assembly.

Section 2.

The Vice President/s shall have the following power,duties and function.

a.Assume that the Presidency in an internim capacity in case the President resigns or otherwise incapacitated.Since threeVice President therein,the board shall elect from one of them.

b.CO-preside with the President over the meetings of the executive committee and the sessions of the general assembly.

c.Assist the President of the active LETS Band Organization.

d.Shall be the organization's representative to the other organization inside the University in case the outside may call a meet with the LETS Band Organization.

e.Shall be the link beetween outside organization and the LETS Band Oganization perform such other duties as maybe assigned to him by the President or by the general assembly.

f.Shall give priority attention to the course's major of tier responsibility.

Section 3.

The Secretary shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Make annual report on activities,plans and accomplishments of the LETS Band Organization which are to be certified by the Presedent and to be submitted to the general assembly.

b.Keep and preserve accurate,permanent written records of the proceedings of all the LETS Band Organization meetings records of members and other papers regarding the activities of the LETS Band Organization.

c.Be responsible for all the correspondence of the LETS Band Organization.

d.Be responsible for all the notices of organization meetings and any correspondent's incident to his office.

e.Serve notices of meetings to officers and duplicate and distribute the minutes of the meeting.

f.Perform such other duties and functions as may be delegated to him by the President or the general assembly.

Section 4.

The Treasurer shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Recieve and maintain custody of all funds and properties of the LETS Band Organization and keep and accurate,permanent written records thereof.

b.Collect all fees and make all payments of organizational upon proper Autorization from the President or from the representative properly deignated by the general assembly.

c.Submit financial report to the General Assembly during its session and to executives committee in its by-monthly meetings.

d.Perform such others duties and functions as may be delegated to him by the President or the general assembly.

Section 5.

The Auditor shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Audit the book of the LETS Band Organization semiannually and submit a written report on such audit to the general assembly.

b.Include a certification on the semi-annual report to the effect that has audited the book of the LETS Band Organization:and that the statements reflect the correct financial status of the LETS Band Organization.

c.Recommends measures for the improvement of accounting procedures and for the prevention of irregularly.

d.Perform such other duties and function as may be delegated to him by the President or the general assembly.

Section 6.

The Business Managers shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Will be in charge of Purchasing all necessary items for the current events or projects.

b.Maintain organization database.

c.Completes the chapter's application form.

Section 7.

The PIO shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Handles all matters concerning this dissemination of information to the public.

b.handles liaision work between and among LETS Band Organization chapters as delegated by the President.

c.Manages all publications of the chapter.

Section 8.

Each Representative shall have the following powers,duties and function.

a.Shall serve as a communication link between the Executive Committee and the year he is representing.

b.To decide,on behalf of the group or body he represents which require immediate decisions, and perform such other duties as may be assigned to him by the President or by the general assembly.

Article X

QUALIFICATION OF OFFICERS

Any bona fide of members of the LETS Band Organization qualifications herein enumerated is eligible to hold any position in the Executive Committee, to with.

a.Currently enrolled at Isabela State University of San Mateo.

b.Good moral character, as certified the office of the student affairs.

c.Having established residence in the University school year immediately processing the election.

Article XI

ACCOUNTABILTY OF OFFICERS AND MEMBERS

Section 1.

All officers and members of the organization shall serve the highest degree of responsibility,integrity,loyalty and efficiency and shall be accountable to the LETS Band Organization.

Section 2.

Any officers of the LETS Band Organization shall be removed from the office by the impeachment for will full violation of this constitution and by-Laws,gross neglect of duties ,and grave offence of misconduct reflecting upon his moral charcter,or membership in any known organization of society.

Section 3.

No officer shall be removed from office by impeachment without the concurrence of atleast two-thirds(2/3)of all members of the general assemblyHowever,the officer being tried shall not participate in any way in the impeachment proceedings.

Section 4.

In any case wherein an elected officer will be removed from officer due to disqualification,the remaining body of the Executive Committee with the consent of the advisers of faculties has the right to appoint a new to assume his post.

Article XII

ADVISER

Section 1.

The adviser shall give general advice and guidance to the Organization.

Section 2.The adviser shall review all authorized activities

Section 3.

The adviser shall be the official spokesman to the University Administration.

Section4

The adviser shall perform such other functions as may be provided by proper university authorities.

Article XII

FUNDS

Section 1

LETS Band Organization funds shall be used to purchase projects and other needs related to IT activities only.

Article XIV

ELECTION

Section 1.

Election of officers of the LETS Band Organization shall be physical voting

Section 2.

The outgoing of the LETS Band Organization Executive Committee shall be established procedures on election.

Article XV

AMENDMENTS

Section 1.

This Constitution and By-Laws can be amended every school year by a vote of two-thirds(2/3) of all present members in a regular or special meeting specifically called for that purpose.
